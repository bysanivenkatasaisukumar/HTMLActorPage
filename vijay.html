Vijay

Vijay, also known as Thalapathy Vijay, is an Indian actor, dancer, playback singer, and philanthropist who predominantly works in Tamil cinema. He was born on June 22, 1974, in Chennai, Tamil Nadu, India, to film director S. A. Chandrasekhar and playback singer Shoba Chandrasekhar. His sister, Vidhya Chandrasekhar, who died at the age of two, had a significant impact on him, and he named his production house V.V. Productions in her memory
